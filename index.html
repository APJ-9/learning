    Mi Mobiles - Where Trend Meets Performance

    There is nothing more annoying than getting stuck on one page while browsing the internet. Imagine you are chatting with your friends on Whatsapp, and want to send something interesting that you came across on Facebook, and by the time you share it, your phone hangs and you have to wait for a couple of minutes just to load a page. Well, this is a sign that your phone needs an update. Switch to Mi mobiles and experience a glitch-free performance.

    Redmi 9i

    The Redmi 9i smartphone is big on performance and multitasking. This high-performance phone features an immersive 16.58 cm (6.53) display. This large HD+ IPS screen delivers visuals with great clarity and vibrancy, bringing extra fun to your movies, videos, gaming, streaming, and more. You can rely on this Redmi mobile phone to keep you entertained when you are on the go or to work on important work projects, as it packs in a 5000 mAh high capacity battery. With this long-lasting battery, you can enjoy up to 162 hours of music, 25.5 hours of video watching, 11 hours of gaming, and 31 hours of calls on the Redmi 9i. You can ace your photography game and click stunning photographs on this phone, thanks to the AI scene detection technology on the camera. With this feature, you can transform your photos into stunning shots as it automatically adjusts the camera settings. With the 5 MP AI selfie camera, your selfies can look all the more beautiful. You can enjoy improved gaming and connectivity, thanks to the Helio G25 octa-core processor. The 4 GB RAM present in this phone can deliver efficiency and an overall smooth smartphone experience. The phone is also designed to be durable and reliable as it features a splash-proof design, corrosion-proof ports, and rubberised seals that prevent water ingression.

    Redmi 9i Sport

    Work hard and play harder with the Redmi 9i Sport, designed to redefine your phone gaming experience. The MediaTek Helio G25 processor present in the smartphone features a HyperGame Engines Technology that can provide you with sustained gaming performance and enhanced connectivity on your phone. This octa-core processor features dynamic and intelligent GPU, CPU, and memory. The phone has a large HD+ IPS display to help you view content with great clarity. The efficient RAM and abundant storage space enable you to multitask and download apps without worrying. You can find a 13 MP rear camera and a 5 MP front-facing camera on the Redmi 9i Sport that can enable you to click impressive photographs and self-portraits. Convenient features like Palm Shutter help make it easy for you to capture joyous moments in a matter of seconds. In addition to the impressive array of features, this Redmi mobile phone is big on looks, as it features a premium design. The phone's design showcases concentric circles that give it an edgy look. The added grip makes handling the device easy. The phone is also designed to be splash-proof and corrosion-proof, giving it excellent durability.

    Redmi 9 Power

    Experience power-packed performance with the Redmi 9 Power phone that delivers a smooth and fast performance. This phone has a massive battery with a capacity of 6000 mAh to help you stay on top of all your important tasks. The phone features an ultra battery saver mode and supports 18 W fast charging and reverse charging to keep it ready to tackle your tasks at all times. The FHD+ IPS 16.58 cm (6.53) display of this phone can give you a cinematic viewing experience. You can view content with clear image quality with sharp and detailed visuals on the widescreen display of the phone. The 48 MP quad-camera array of this phone helps you seize every special moment with excellent details and clarity. You can capture more in every frame with the ultra-wide lens, capture pictures with added depth with the depth sensor, and capture minute details with the macro lens provided in the quad-camera array. The Redmi 9 Power smartphone comes with stereo speakers to provide superior sound quality to elevate your gaming, movie watching, and video calling experiences. The Qualcomm Snapdragon 662 processor of the phone delivers high performance without consuming a lot of power. This power-efficient processor aids in delivering stunning and detail-rich images on the 48 MP main camera at zero shutter lag.
